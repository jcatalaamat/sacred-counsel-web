## How to Download

### Method 1: Right-Click Save (Easiest)
1. Open the page in Chrome/Firefox
2. Play the video
3. Right-click on the video → "Save video as..."

### Method 2: Browser Dev Tools
1. Open the page in Chrome
2. Press F12 → Network tab
3. Filter by "mp4" or "media"
4. Play the video
5. Find the request to video.wixstatic.com
6. Right-click the request → "Copy as cURL" or "Open in new tab"
7. Download from the authenticated URL

### Method 3: Browser Extension
Use "Video DownloadHelper" extension for Firefox/Chrome
